EmailJS Setup

Step 1: Create an EmailJS Account

  1. Go to https://dashboard.emailjs.com/sign-up
  2. Click Sign Up to create an account
  3. Fill out the form:
    • Enter your name
    • Provide your email address
    • Set a secure password
    • Complete the CAPTCHA verification
  4. Click Sign Up to complete the process

Step 2: Log in to EmailJS

  1. Go to https://dashboard.emailjs.com/sign-in
  2. Enter the email address and password you used to sign up
  3. If you forget your password, click Forgot your password? to reset it
  4. Click Sign In to access your dashboard

Step 3: Confirm Your Email Address

  1. After signing up, EmailJS will send a confirmation email to the address you provided
  2. Check your inbox for the confirmation email
  3. If you don’t see it, check your Spam or Junk folder
  4. Click the activation link in the email to verify your account
  5. If you don’t receive the email, click the Resend Email button shown on this page
  6. Sign in again to your EmailJS account after confirming your email

Step 4: Add an Email Service

  1. From the EmailJS Dashboard, navigate to Email Services in the left menu.
  2. Click Add New Service to configure your email provider.
  3. Choose your provider (e.g., Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) or a transactional service (e.g., SendGrid, Mailgun).
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to link your email account to EmailJS.
  5. After selecting an email service (e.g., Gmail), you’ll see a configuration form.
  6. Enter a name for the service (e.g., "Gmail").
  7. The Service ID will be auto-generated.
  8. Click Connect Account and follow the prompts to authorize EmailJS to send emails on your behalf.
  9. Ensure the box for Send test email to verify configuration is checked.
  10. Click Create Service to finalize the setup.
